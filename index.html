Image → ASCII

Every photo is just light and dark. This spells it out in characters.

Drop in a photo. Dark pixels become dense characters, bright pixels become sparse ones, until the whole image is rebuilt out of text. Runs entirely in your browser, nothing is uploaded anywhere.
